The successful story of Serva-Lite

Serva-Lite Sales Ltd. has been serving the emergency lighting and fire alarm markets since 1986. We are a specialized electrical wholesale distributor providing specialized products and services to Contractors, School Districts and Fire Alarm companies in British Columbia and Alberta. We pride ourselves as a stocking distributor, as well as providing technical support to our customers.

We started to offer lighting products from the leading manufacturers in 2019. Our clients order the lighting for industrial, commercial, and residential facilities.

Lilian Ortiz-Davila

Outside sales

Lilian brings over 15 years’ experience to Serva-Lite where she leads the Lighting department. Her passion for Lighting Design and product knowledge creates a unique blend of customer service and detail to the Lighting requirements of a job. Lilian has worked collaboratively on a variety of projects from indoor to outdoor applications. Working closely with Architects, Landscape Architects, Lighting Designers, and Engineers in Canada, and Mexico. Her friendly approach and international experience translate a concept into a solution for the contractor with a unique and holistic approach.
Trained as an Architect, Lilian has specialized in Lighting and studied a post education in Lighting Design in Mexico and the Netherlands getting his LC certification in 2018. She is frequent speaker in various Lighting Forums, and you will find her teaching and inspiring students in various Colleges and Universities in BC. Member of the IES Vancouver, and active volunteer instructor for the Education Committee.
Through her passion and expertise Lilian understands the power of Light in the space and the huge impact that creates in the human experience. Her understanding of this facts and her expertise has made her a valuable team in the contractor business.
Lilian blends her passion for Lighting with her passion for traveling with her family. They usually around in trade shows around the world, and Lighting is already part of the vocabulary of her children. She is a Light chaser, traveler, foody, dog lover and a mom.
