The successful story of Serva-Lite

Serva-Lite Sales Ltd. has been serving the emergency lighting and fire alarm markets since 1986. We are a specialized electrical wholesale distributor providing specialized products and services to Contractors, School Districts and Fire Alarm companies in British Columbia and Alberta. We pride ourselves as a stocking distributor, as well as providing technical support to our customers.

We started to offer lighting products from the leading manufacturers in 2019. Our clients order the lighting for industrial, commercial, and residential facilities.
